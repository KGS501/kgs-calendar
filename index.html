KGS Calendar Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Overview

KGS Calendar is a calendar and task app. It has no ads, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or developer-operated backend service.

Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores calendar events, tasks, reminders, settings, source names, server URLs, usernames, and cached calendar data on your device. Nextcloud app passwords are stored with Android encrypted preferences and are excluded from Android app backup.

Sync And Accounts

If you add a Nextcloud account or an ICS/CalDAV URL, the app sends calendar data, server URLs, usernames, and credentials only to the server or URL you configure so syncing can work. The app does not create a KGS Calendar account.

Location Search And Maps

Location search is optional. When you press Search in the location picker, the search query is sent to OpenStreetMap Nominatim. If you choose nearby results, approximate device location is used to bias that search. Map previews are loaded from OpenStreetMap only after you choose to show a preview.

OpenStreetMap data is provided by OpenStreetMap contributors.

Deleting Local Data

You can remove a calendar source in Settings to delete its local credentials and locally stored data for that source. The server calendar stays unchanged. Uninstalling the app deletes local app data from the device.

Contact

Use the developer contact email shown on the Google Play listing for privacy questions.
